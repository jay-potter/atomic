Photography

The Schweizer 300 is a perfect aerial platform for photography/videography. It has more headroom and cockpit width than the R-22 and R-44, with easily removable doors and plenty of room for all your gear. With the 4-5 hour range the 300C has, you can photograph anything in the Puget Sound area, including ocean, mountains, cities and rural areas.
