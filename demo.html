For just $150 you can begin training!

Most people that are considering helicopter flight training have never flown a helicopter before. To give you an introduction to flying helicopters we offer a 1/2 hour introductory flight at the reduced rate of $150.00 ($225.00 for R44). This is an interactive, hands on flight, where you will be given 1/2 hour of ground introduction to the helicopter, and what makes it fly. After the ground time, you will take off at Boeing Field, and fly either north, around downtown Seattle, or south towards Auburn. Both are beautiful and engaging flights. You will be given the opportunity to fly the helicopter as much or as little as you like, whatever you are comfortable with.

The demo flight is a perfect way to get started with your training, to meet an instructor and familiarize yourself with the helicopter. Give us a call anytime to schedule!
