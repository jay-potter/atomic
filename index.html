Our Services

We provide a wide range of helicopter services from Flight Training to Agricultural work.

Flight Training

We have chosen the Schweizer 300 for our students because of its excellent safety record, and its student friendly flying qualities. Most students are able to progress through their flight training much closer to "on schedule", and therefore on budget in the Schweizer than in other helicopters used for training.

We have both VFR and IFR training helicopters, and instructors with years of experience in both.

Photography

The Schweizer 300 is a perfect aerial platform for photography/videography. It has more headroom and cockpit width than the R-22 and R-44, with easily removable doors and plenty of room for all your gear. With the 4 hour range the 300C has, you can photograph anything in the Puget Sound area, including ocean, mountains, cities and rural areas.
