White River Run and Vashon Island Tour

Depart Boeing Field to the South east towards the Renton Airport and Sea plane base located at the South tip of Lake Washington and overfly beautiful Lake Youngs. We then fly over the 66 acres of Crest Airpark, the cities of Covington and Kent, crossover the Green River and drop into the White River Valley where we slow down to enjoy the twists and turns of the beautiful White River valley. The return flight to Seattle overflies Twin Lake Golf Course across the Sound to Vashon Island. Fly up the coast of Vashon Island and take in the beauty of the Puget Sound before returning to Boeing Field over West Seattle.

The Marinas of the Sound Tour

The Marina tour starts with a Helicopter only departure across the Runways at Boeing Field westbound to Alki beach. We then follow the iconic ferries to Winslow Marina on beautiful Bainbridge Island. As we fly north along the coast, your pilot will point out the many water front homes facing Seattle. At Fay Bainbridge park we turn east back across the water to Shilshole Marine and Discovery Park. You can then see the boats going through the Ballard Locks on our way to the University of Washington Campus and Stadium. We will then fly over the house boats on Lake Union, Gasworks Park and make our way to the Space Needle, the Waterfront, and Stadiums.
